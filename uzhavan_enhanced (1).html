🛡️ Crop Insurance Schemes / பயிர் காப்பீடு திட்டங்கள்
🌾
PMFBY – Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
Comprehensive crop insurance covering pre-sowing to post-harvest losses. Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for commercial crops.
Claim ProcessOnline / Offline
🌧️
RWBCIS – Weather Based Crop Insurance
Covers losses from adverse weather — excess/deficit rain, frost, heat wave. Payout triggered by weather index thresholds.
CoverageWeather Index
🏛️
TNAU Crop Insurance (Tamil Nadu)
State scheme for paddy, maize, groundnut, cotton and other TN crops. Operated through cooperative banks and primary societies.
Apply atBlock Agri Office
